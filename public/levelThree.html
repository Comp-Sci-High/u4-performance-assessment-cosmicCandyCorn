Advanced Breakfast Ideas

Japanese Soufflé Pancakes

Cost: $$$

How to Make:

  • Separate egg yolks and whites; beat whites to stiff peaks.
  • Mix yolks with sugar, milk, and flour.
  • Gently fold egg whites into the batter.
  • Cook pancakes on low heat with a lid on.
  • Serve with whipped cream and syrup.

Fun Fact: These fluffy pancakes originated in Japan and became a viral sensation!

Homemade Croissants

Cost: $$$

How to Make:

  • Mix flour, sugar, yeast, and milk to form dough.
  • Fold butter into the dough and laminate it with multiple layers.
  • Refrigerate and roll out dough multiple times.
  • Shape into croissants and let them proof.
  • Bake until golden and flaky.

Fun Fact: Croissants originated in Austria, not France!

Lobster Benedict

Cost: $$$

How to Make:

  • Poach lobster meat in butter.
  • Make hollandaise sauce using egg yolks, butter, and lemon.
  • Poach eggs in simmering water.
  • Toast English muffins and top with lobster and eggs.
  • Drizzle with hollandaise and serve.

Fun Fact: Lobster Benedict is a luxurious twist on the classic Eggs Benedict.

Breakfast Beef Wellington

Cost: $$$

How to Make:

  • Season and sear beef filet.
  • Layer with mushroom duxelles and wrap in puff pastry.
  • Bake until golden and crispy.
  • Prepare scrambled eggs and serve on the side.
  • Garnish with herbs and hollandaise sauce.

Fun Fact: Beef Wellington was named after the Duke of Wellington!

Quiche Lorraine

Cost: $$$

How to Make:

  • Prepare a buttery shortcrust pastry.
  • Mix eggs, cream, cheese, and cooked bacon.
  • Pour into the crust and bake until set.
  • Let it cool slightly before slicing.
  • Serve warm or cold.

Fun Fact: Quiche Lorraine is a French classic that dates back to the 16th century.

Smoked Salmon Tartine

Cost: $$$

How to Make:

  • Toast artisan bread slices.
  • Spread cream cheese and fresh herbs.
  • Layer smoked salmon and thinly sliced onions.
  • Drizzle with lemon juice and capers.
  • Serve with microgreens on top.

Fun Fact: Tartines are open-faced sandwiches popular in France.
